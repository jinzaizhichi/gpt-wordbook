### 分析词义

不接受申请或提议（rejected the offer）；身体排斥（rejected the transplant）；文件被退回（rejected application）。形容词 a rejected manuscript 被拒稿。

<Pronunciation word="Rejected" uk="[rɪˈdʒektɪd]" us="[rɪˈdʒektɪd]" client:only />

### 列举例句

1. "The bank rejected his loan application due to low credit score." (银行因信用分低拒绝了他的贷款申请。)
2. "Her article was rejected but she revised it and resubmitted." (她的文章被拒，但她修改后再次投稿。)
3. "The immune system rejected the foreign tissue." (免疫系统排斥了外来组织。)

### 词根分析

拉丁语 reicere，re-（回）+ iacere（扔），与 project（向前扔）、eject（弹出）同源。

### 词缀分析

re-（回）+ ject（扔）+ -ed；名词 rejection，形容词 reject 作名词指“被拒者/次品”。

### 文化背景

求职 rejected after the interview；科技 payment rejected 支付失败。心理 feeling rejected 被排斥感。

### 单词变形

- 固定搭配: "rejected application" (被拒申请), "feel rejected" (感到被拒绝), "rejected proposal" (被否方案)
- 名词: rejection

### 记忆辅助

re- 扔回去，ject 是扔 → rejected 是“已经被扔回来了/不要了”。

### 助记故事

"The editor rejected the draft politely and suggested clearer sources." (编辑礼貌地退回了草稿，并建议补充更清晰的来源。)
