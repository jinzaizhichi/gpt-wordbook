### 分析词义

代表他人或组织（lawyer representing the client）；象征（The logo representing unity）；数据/图表 representing trends 呈现趋势；参赛 representing your country 代表国家出战。

### 列举例句

1. "She is representing the union in contract talks." (她在合同谈判中代表工会。)
2. "Each bar on the chart is representing sales by region." (图表每根柱表示各区域销售额。)
3. "Athletes representing Japan marched into the stadium." (代表日本的运动员列队入场。)

### 词根分析

拉丁语 repraesentare，re-（再次）+ praesentare（呈现、使在场），与 present 同源。

### 词缀分析

re- + present + -ing；名词 representation，形容词 representative。

### 文化背景

法律 representing counsel 代理律师；外交 representing interests 代表利益。艺术 representing reality 再现现实。

### 单词变形

- 固定搭配: "representing clients" (代理客户), "representing interests" (代表利益), "self-representing" (自我代理)
- 名词: representation

### 记忆辅助

re- 再次 + present 呈现 → 把某人/某事“再次摆到台前”就是代表。

### 助记故事

"The firm is representing several startups in licensing negotiations this quarter." (该公司本季度在许可谈判中代理多家初创企业。)
