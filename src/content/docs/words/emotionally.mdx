### 分析词义

可描述状态（emotionally exhausted）、支持方式（emotionally available）或行为方式（react emotionally）。与 "rationally" 常形成对照。

<Pronunciation word="Emotionally" uk="[ɪˈməʊʃənəli]" us="[ɪˈmoʊʃənəli]" client:only />

### 列举例句

1. "She was emotionally drained after caring for her parents." (照顾父母后她在情感上耗尽了。)
2. "He tends to respond emotionally when he feels criticized." (他觉得被批评时往往情绪化反应。)
3. "The speech was emotionally powerful without being manipulative." (这场演讲感人有力却不煽情操控。)

### 词根分析

拉丁语 emovere（激起、移动：e-「出」+ movere「动」）→ "emotion" + "-al" + "-ly"。

### 词缀分析

"emotional" + 副词后缀 "-ly"。

### 文化背景

心理健康讨论中 "emotionally intelligent" 为常见褒义搭配；职场语境有时提醒 "don't take it emotionally" 指别过度代入情绪。

### 单词变形

- 固定搭配: "emotionally intelligent" (情商高的), "emotionally stable" (情绪稳定的)
- 形容词: emotional；名词: emotion

### 记忆辅助

emotion（情绪）+ ally（以…方式）→ 用「心情」而不是纯逻辑在应对。

### 助记故事

"The reunion was emotionally intense for siblings who had lived apart for years." (多年分居的兄弟姐妹重逢，场面在情感上非常浓烈。)
