### 分析词义

物理覆盖（covering the table with a cloth）；新闻 covering the election 报道选举；范围 covering all regions 涵盖所有地区；保险 covering medical costs 承保医疗费用。

<Pronunciation word="Covering" uk="[ˈkʌvərɪŋ]" us="[ˈkʌvərɪŋ]" client:only />

### 列举例句

1. "Reporters are covering the summit live from Geneva." (记者正从日内瓦现场报道峰会。)
2. "Snow was covering the rooftops by morning." (早晨雪覆盖了屋顶。)
3. "The warranty is covering parts but not labor." (保修涵盖零件但不涵盖人工。)

### 词根分析

古法语 covrir，拉丁语 cooperire（co- 一起 + operire 盖上），与 discover（揭开）相对。

### 词缀分析

cover + -ing；cover 可作名词“封面、掩护”，covering letter 附信。

### 文化背景

媒体 beat covering tech 科技条线；金融 covering short positions 平仓掩护空头。日常 covering for a colleague 代班。

### 单词变形

- 固定搭配: "covering the story" (报道事件), "covering costs" (支付费用), "covering for someone" (替人顶班)
- 原形: cover；过去式: covered

### 记忆辅助

cover 是“盖住”，-ing 是“正在盖/正在报道/正在包括”。

### 助记故事

"She spent the week covering city council meetings for the local paper." (她这周为当地报纸报道市议会会议。)
