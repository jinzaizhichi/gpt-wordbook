### 分析词义

"Treating" 是 **treat** 的 **-ing** 形式。常见义：**对待**（**treating people fairly**）、**医治**（**treating patients**）、**请客**（**treating friends to dinner**）、**处理**（**treating wood with oil**）。

<Pronunciation word="Treating" uk="[ˈtriːtɪŋ]" us="[ˈtriːtɪŋ]" client:only />

### 列举例句

1. "The clinic specializes in treating chronic pain." (该诊所专长治疗慢性疼痛。)
2. "She apologized for not treating his concerns seriously." (她为没有认真对待他的担忧而道歉。)
3. "He is treating the team to pizza after the win." (获胜后他请全队吃披萨。)

### 词根分析

**treat** 来自古法语 **traitier**（处理、谈判），与拉丁语 **tractare**（处理）相关。

### 词缀分析

**treat** + **-ing** 表进行；名词 **treatment** 表治疗或对待方式。

### 文化背景

医疗语境 **treating symptoms** 与 **treating root causes** 常对比；社交 **treating someone** 含请客、优待意味。

### 单词变形

- 固定搭配: "treating patients" (医治病人), "treating fairly" (公平对待), "treat with" (用…处理/对待)

### 记忆辅助

**treat** 像 **“款待 + 处理”** 一体 → **treating** 就是正在款待或正在医治。

### 助记故事

"While treating a sprained ankle, the coach kept treating every player with respect, win or lose." (在处理脚踝扭伤的同时，教练无论输赢都对每位队员保持尊重。)
